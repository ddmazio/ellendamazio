A to B

The Path of Wayfinding Systems in the UK
Editorial Design
2023

A to B – The Path of Wayfinding Systems in the UK explores how iconic design legacies can be preserved and reinterpreted for contemporary audiences. Centered on the work of Margaret Calvert and Jock Kinneir—pioneers of British road signage—the project is both a documentation and a visual homage to their influence on modern information design and public space navigation.

The outcome is a printed editorial piece that reflects the clarity of their original system while offering a fresh editorial perspective. By combining typographic hierarchy, disciplined grids, and a restrained color palette drawn from British signage, the design honors their minimalist ethos. Subtle shifts in layout and pacing bring contemporary rhythm to the work, bridging legacy and relevance.
