UNPROMPT

Branding
2025

UNPROMPT was born from a central paradox in contemporary design — in an era where artificial intelligence offers speed, automation, and boundless creative possibilities, critical questions remain: Who owns the output? What is lost in translation? And how do we ensure that technology amplifies, rather than replaces, the creative process?

The event opens with UNHUMAN: Setting the Stage, a roundtable moderated by creative duo Boldtron, featuring Mustafa Suleyman (co-founder of DeepMind and author of The Coming Wave) and cognitive scientist Gary Marcus, one of AI’s most vocal critics. Together, they explore the risks, limits, and potential of AI in creativity. Next comes UNCLAIM: Art in the Hands of Machines, a talk by Jessica Walsh (&Walsh) addressing the unauthorized use of creative works in AI training datasets, and the urgent need for ethical and legal frameworks to protect artistic authorship. Finally, UNFILTER: From Sketch to Synth brings theory into action through a live creative workshop, where Boldtron builds an artwork from concept to final render in just three hours — offering a transparent look into how AI can enhance, not erase, the human touch.

From critical thinkers to radical makers, UNPROMPT offers a 360° exploration of AI in design — blending ethical reflection, authorship advocacy, and hands-on experimentation. Join us to question, explore, and redefine the role of artificial intelligence — not as a replacement, but as a sensitive, transparent co-creator in the future of creativity. This project was developed within the Communication Design IV module of the Communication Design program at FBAUL.
