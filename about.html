Let's Talk!

Hi, I'm Ellen Damazio.

After trying out different career paths and fields of study, I found my sweet spot in Communication Design—a space where my love for design, art, and tech all come together. It's where I can truly express my creativity while diving into the technical side of things, making it the perfect fit for me.

My journey has been all over the place, but in the best way possible. Along the way, I co-founded an NGO that helps young Brazilians in public schools discover their potential and grab new opportunities. This experience really showed me how powerful design can be in creating positive change and making a real difference. Through my work, I'm always blending design, art, and technology to come up with fresh, impactful solutions.

Currently, I'm volunteering at Vitalis Project ↗ and always looking for new projects.
