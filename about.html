Hi, I’m Ellen. I’m a multidisciplinary designer with a love for interaction, code, and visual craft — and a real curiosity for where design meets emerging technologies.

I’m currently finishing my Communication Design degree at FBAUL, and expanding my practice through freelance projects, research, and collaborations with people who are deeply passionate about what they do.

Outside of design, you’ll find me watching movies and series, trying out new recipes, building elaborate houses in the Sims 4, or out with my camera.

Always feel free to reach out with cool project ideas (or for coffee) ☺️
