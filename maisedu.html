Maisedu

Visual Identity
2024

Maisedu was a nonprofit organization committed to transforming education for students in Brazil's public school system. Rooted in the United Nations' Sustainable Development Goals, it delivered over 20 free initiatives — from extracurricular workshops to educational events — directly supporting the personal and academic growth of young people often left behind by traditional systems.

The rebranding aimed to visually align the organization's mission with its modern, youth-driven, and socially conscious values. The new identity blends bold, optimistic colors with a modular, flexible layout system and human-centered typography, capturing Brazilian students' energy and the clarity of purpose behind educational equity.

More than just a visual update, this identity became a strategic tool for engagement and recognition. It positioned Maisedu as a forward-thinking, action-oriented initiative that not only talks about impact, but delivers it — by creating real, lasting change where it's needed most.
