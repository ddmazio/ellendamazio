Azucrinação

Branding
2025

Azucrinação is a limited-edition music anthology that asks: how can graphic design preserve the memory of artistic resistance under oppressive regimes? Created to honor Brazil's democratic history, the project merges the visual rawness of post-punk with the lyrical defiance of MPB, whose metaphor and irony challenged Brazil's military dictatorship (1970–1985).

Packaged as a multisensory collector's box, the project includes two curated vinyls and a World Cup-inspired sticker album that weaves lyrics, stories, and emotions into a playful, tactile archive.

The outcome is a printed editorial piece that reflects the clarity of their original system while offering a fresh editorial perspective. By combining typographic hierarchy, disciplined grids, and a restrained color palette drawn from British signage, the design honors their minimalist ethos. Subtle shifts in layout and pacing bring contemporary rhythm to the work, bridging legacy and relevance.
